White wine salmon spaghetti

Feb. 18th 2014

Mentioned Pasta / Pasta face imagine how we like it? General usually eat face called .. "spaghetti" is actually fine Pasta kind .. there are many angels face (fine), Linguini (wide ), Fettuccine (extra wide), and so on, depending on its width, this time I'll do it yourself with a spaghetti noodle-making machine! usually do manual pasta I would choose a little wide of the surface, so the child can enjoy pasta their taste & texture. practice is actually more than we imagined yet simple! (although this step a lot ..) basically as long as the flour mixture with the egg and then roll around & good cut everything done! noodle machine if there is such a word , relatively smooth process. direct hand roll & cut can do.
